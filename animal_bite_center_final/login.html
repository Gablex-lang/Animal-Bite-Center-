What is Rabies?
A deadly virus spread through the bite or scratch of an infected animal — most commonly dogs. Once symptoms appear, it is almost always fatal. That is why immediate treatment after a bite is critical.
Who We Are
The Animal Bite Center is a DOH-accredited facility dedicated to treating people bitten by animals suspected of carrying rabies. We provide immediate medical care, proper wound management, and the complete anti-rabies vaccination series to prevent the virus from taking hold.
Why Come to Us?
Rabies is 100% preventable if treated early. Our center acts fast — we assess the wound, wash and dress the bite, administer anti-rabies vaccines, and guide every patient through their full PEP schedule so no dose is ever missed.
Act Within 24 Hours
If you or someone you know has been bitten by a dog, cat, or any wild animal — do not wait. Wash the wound immediately with soap and water, then come to us right away. Time is the most important factor in preventing rabies.

Who We Are

Committed to Your Recovery & Safety

The Animal Bite Center provides fast and reliable care for bite injuries, rabies prevention, and health consultations for the people of Cagayan de Oro City.

Our dedicated medical team responds swiftly to animal bite emergencies with evidence-based post-exposure prophylaxis (PEP) protocols. We are equipped with licensed vaccines, trained professionals, and a streamlined digital records system to ensure no patient is missed.

Licensed under the Department of Health (DOH), we follow the National Rabies Prevention and Control Program guidelines to deliver safe, effective, and affordable treatment to our community.
